But through the dedicated support of monthly and recurring donors like you, Humanitary Relief Wave is on the front lines, doing whatever it takes to protect children in the U.S. and around the world.

Thanks to your recurring donations, we've achieved tremendous results

  • Through Humanitary Relief Wave’s work – every second of every day – a hungry child is fed, a sick child gets care, a young mind is learning, a vulnerable child is protected, or a family gains tools to overcome poverty.
  • Since our founding over 100 years ago, we’ve changed the lives of over 1 billion children.
  • Since the start of the COVID-19 pandemic, globally, we’ve trained or supported 95,000 health workers and provided 762,000 families with access to safe water
  • We’ve helped 9 million children access quality education across the world, including more than 4.5 million girls.
  • Across America, we’ve delivered nearly 5 million nourishing meals to children in poor, rural communities.

What Makes Save the Children a Children’s Charity You Can Trust?

Humanitary Relief Wave is devoted solely to serving children’s needs and securing their rights. This lifesaving work has been recognized by charity watch groups.

Recently, we were proud to receive our 16th four-star rating from Charity Navigator for sound fiscal management and a proven commitment to accountability and transparency.

You can rest assured that your charity donation to Save the Children is one of the world’s best investments. In fact, we keep administrative costs low so that more funding goes to children’s programs.

86% of every dollar you give goes to programs and services for children and families in need.
